1,000 random words from a 100k+ dictionary, regenerated daily. A writing tool for getting random words in your head.

filters
Press f to open the filter menu. Filter words by mood (joy, sadness, anger...), part of speech, syllable count, or sentiment. Filters can be combined. Each option has a hotkey.
data sources
Emotions from the NRC Word-Emotion Association Lexicon. Syllable counts from the CMU Pronouncing Dictionary. Part of speech extracted heuristically from definitions.
